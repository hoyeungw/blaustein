**(Etiological Engine: Primitive Scientific Urge)**

(∫未知现象(unknown phenomenon)↑ → 神话生成(mythogenesis)↑) ⇄⁺ (社会知识缺口(societal knowledge gap) ~⁺ 叙事复杂度(narrative complexity))
